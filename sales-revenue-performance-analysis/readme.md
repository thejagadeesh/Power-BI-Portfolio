## Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive **Sales Performance Dashboard** built in Power BI for **GUVI**. The analysis tracks revenue trends, user demographics, and conversion metrics across two primary currency segments: **INR** and **USD**. The dashboard provides actionable insights into product popularity, marketing channel efficiency, and regional user behavior.
![--](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/andreasbm/readme/master/assets/lines/rainbow.png)

## 🔍 Data-Driven Insights

### 1. Channel Attribution & Effectiveness
* **Direct Dominance:** Direct traffic is the primary driver, accounting for **80.13% (INR)** and **85.5% (USD)** of total sales.
* **Influencer Impact:** Influencer-led sales are significantly more impactful in the USD market (**10.96%**) compared to Email marketing (**3.55%**).
* **Refund Risk:** Data indicates that **Email** sources carry a higher propensity for refunds relative to their volume.

### 2. Product & Linguistic Trends
* **Top Products (INR):** Product1, Product4, and Product3 are the highest revenue generators.
* **Top Products (USD):** Product1 remains #1, followed by Product18 and Product10.
* **Linguistic Demographics:** * **INR:** English (3,577) and Tamil (3,318) are the leading user languages.
    * **USD:** Tamil leads in the USD segment, indicating a strong international Tamil-speaking user base.

### 3. Sales Operations & Velocity
* **Payment Preferences:** **UPI** is the undisputed leader in India (**1,184 transactions**), while **Card** payments are the exclusive mode for USD transactions.
* **Conversion Speed:** The "Lead to Sale" metric confirms that **8,141 users** convert on **Day 0**. The probability of conversion drops drastically if the sale is not closed within 24 hours.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Visualization:** Power BI Desktop (Interactive Slicers, Tooltips, Multi-currency filtering).
* **Data Modeling:** DAX calculations for Conversion %, Refund Rates, and Sales Trends.
* **Key Features:** Daily Sales Trend analysis, Payment Mode breakdown, and Language-based user segmentation.

![--](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/andreasbm/readme/master/assets/lines/rainbow.png)

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
1. **Optimize USD Funnel:** Address the low USD conversion rate (1.19%) by investigating international payment friction or localized pricing strategies.
2. **Double Down on Influencers:** Reallocate a portion of the international marketing budget from Email to Influencer partnerships, as they show higher ROI.
3. **Immediate Retargeting:** Implement automated "Day 0" retargeting campaigns (push notifications/emails) to capture users within the critical first 2 hours of sign-up.
